Design

Westchester Family Care

WFC needed to update the content and strategy of their site, so they decided to update the design as well. They wanted to have a very modern looking site, following the latest design trends, but I felt it was also important that they also try and capture a ‘grandmother’s house’ feel, so I included watercolor illustrations to bring to mind the art in grandmother’s house (maybe done by a grandchild), and put the images askew to make the site seem more personable.

Designed mainly in Adobe XD, with some Photoshop and Illustrator touches as well.

One-Off

Industrial Metal Weight Calculator

This JavaScript calculator computes the weight of sheets or rods of 4 different metals given the dimensions that a customer inputs. The calculations can be adjusted on the fly including, the units of measurement and number of units. As inputs are entered, the results summary mirrors the answers, and once enough fields have been entered a weight is calculated.

The calculator is written in JavaScript, and it creates cookies on the site based on the user's input that get picked up to pre-populate the contact form.
